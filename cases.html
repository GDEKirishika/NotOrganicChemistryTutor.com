Trigonometric Integration Roadmap

CASE 1Sin/Cos ODD

Example: ∫ sin³(x) dx

∫ (1 - cos²x) sin(x) dx
u = cos(x), du = -sin(x) dx
Ans: -cos(x) + cos³(x)/3 + C

CASE 2Sin/Cos EVEN

Example: ∫ cos²(x) dx

∫ (1 + cos 2x)/2 dx
Ans: x/2 + sin(2x)/4 + C

CASE 1 (Sec/Tan)Sec/Csc EVEN

Example: ∫ sec⁴(x) dx

Isolate sec²x: ∫ (1 + tan²x) sec²x dx
u = tan(x), du = sec²x dx
Ans: tan(x) + tan³(x)/3 + C

SPECIALProduct-to-Sum (sin cos, sin sin, cos cos)

Example: ∫ sin(5x)sin(3x) dx

Use: ½[cos(A-B) - cos(A+B)]
½ ∫ (cos 2x - cos 8x) dx
Ans: sin(2x)/4 - sin(8x)/16 + C
Powers of SIN & COS
Case 1: sin or cos ODD
sin²x=1-cos²x | cos²x=1-sin²x
Case 2: sin/cos EVEN
sin²x=(1-cos2x)/2 | cos²x=(1+cos2x)/2
Case 3: sin/cos ODD (Combined)
Save the smaller power and substitute.
Case 4: sin/cos EVEN (Combined)
Apply power-reducing to both.
Powers of SEC & TAN
Case 1: sec or csc EVEN
sec²x = 1 + tan²x
Case 2: tan or cot isolate
Isolate one factor to use u-sub.
Case 3: Sec/Tan isolate sec²
Identify du = sec²x.
